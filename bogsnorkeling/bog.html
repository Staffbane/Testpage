Membership

Whilst the Sports Hall is open to the public becomming a member has its advantages. Membership is dependent on the following criteria

  1. You live in the Parish of Webberbridge
  2. You are a member of a club which uses the Webberbridge Sports Hall
  3. You have family connection with someone who meets criteria 1 or 2
  4. You have paid in full to use the Webberbridge Sports Halls at least 3 times in the past 3 months
